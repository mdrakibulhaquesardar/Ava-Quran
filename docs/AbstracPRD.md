# Ava Quran — Features Overview

## What is Ava Quran?

Ava Quran is a modern mobile app designed to help people stay connected with the Quran in a simple, calm, and meaningful way after Ramadan.

Instead of long reading sessions, Ava provides short personalized Quran moments that fit naturally into everyday life.

---

# Core Features

## Personalized Quran Feed

A smooth vertical feed of Quran verses tailored to the user’s mood, interests, and habits.

Each card includes:

* Quran verse
* translation
* recitation audio
* short reflection
* calming experience

---

## Mood-Based Quran Experience

Users can select how they feel:

* stressed
* anxious
* grateful
* hopeful
* distracted
* peaceful

Ava then shows Quran verses that emotionally match the moment.

---

## Audio-First Experience

Beautiful Quran recitations play directly inside the feed, making engagement easy even during busy moments.

---

## Short Daily Quran Moments

Quick Quran sessions designed for modern attention spans.

Even 1 minute is enough to reconnect with the Quran.

---

## AI-Powered Reflections

Each verse includes a short, simple insight that helps users understand and reflect on the meaning in daily life.

---

## Save & Organize Favorite Ayahs

Users can:

* bookmark verses
* create collections
* organize ayahs by themes

Example:

* Comfort
* Motivation
* Patience
* Anxiety Relief

---

## Reflection Journal

Users can write private reflections about verses and revisit them later.

---

## Daily Streaks & Consistency

Gentle habit-building tools help users stay consistent with Quran engagement without feeling overwhelming.

---

## Smart Search

Search Quran by:

* topic
* feeling
* keyword
* life situation

Example:

* patience
* stress
* gratitude
* forgiveness

---

## Calm & Modern Design

A peaceful, minimal mobile experience inspired by modern apps while maintaining a respectful Islamic aesthetic.

---

# Main Experience

Ava Quran transforms Quran engagement into:

* simple
* personal
* peaceful
* emotionally meaningful
* easy to maintain daily

---

# Vision

To help Muslims build a lasting daily relationship with the Quran through modern, accessible, and reflective experiences.
